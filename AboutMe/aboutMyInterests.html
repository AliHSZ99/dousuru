My Interests

I love playing Basketball. In the Philippines, basketball is not only considered as a sport, it also acts as a religion. This is the most popular sport among the Filipinos, which almost everyone practices and plays this religious-like sport. Anywhere once can think of, there is always a person or a group of people playing this game; at the beach, on the streets no matter how good or bad the weather is, nothing can stop the Filipinos from playing this sport.
